Convention of exported Postman requests

Before exporting your collection and uploading it below (which you can find the how-to export right here). Please make sure that your Postman requests follows the following convention:

  • BED Assignment is the collection name
  • Discounts, Users, Categories are the folders that group the collections and the requests inside the folders are the action of the request (e.g. Insert a new user) VERY IMPORTANT!!!
